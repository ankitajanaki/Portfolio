PHILANTROPHY


Definition:

phi-lan-thro-py
fə'lanTHrəpē/

noun
"the desire to promote the welfare of others, expressed especially by the generous donation of money to good causes."




In Your Community:

You can make your community a better place in a plethora of ways. Some examples are volunteering at soup kitchens, tutoring other students, and raising money for causes you are passionate about. Several types of groups benefit in different ways from giving back to their communities.

Companies:

1. Respect and a good reputation in the community
2. Make your community a better place to live
3. Employees respect leaders that aid the community
4. Connections and networking
